# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `ai-java-sdk` to be a respectful and productive open source project. Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to communicate professionally and make technical disagreements constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Focus feedback on the code, design, documentation, or behavior being discussed.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Give maintainers enough context to reproduce issues and review contributions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated disruption of technical discussions.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing credentials, secrets, private prompts, or sensitive operational data in public issues or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block accounts, or take other reasonable action when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

To report a concern, contact the repository maintainer privately through the repository owner profile. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context where possible.
